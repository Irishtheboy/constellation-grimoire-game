# Projectile Images

Place your custom projectile images in this folder:

## Required Files:
- `fireball.png` - Fire spell projectile
- `iceshard.png` - Ice spell projectile  
- `lightning.png` - Lightning spell projectile
- `windblast.png` - Wind spell projectile
- `sword.png` - Slash attack projectile
- `healorb.png` - Healing spell projectile
- `barrier.png` - Shield spell projectile
- `default.png` - Fallback projectile

## Image Requirements:
- Format: PNG with transparency
- Size: 80x80 pixels recommended
- Style: Match your game's art style
- Background: Transparent
